Empowering Africa’s healthcare system through cutting-edge technology

HealthStation is empowering Africa’s healthcare system through cutting-edge technology with instant access to data and intelligence for the effective delivery of healthcare services.

As the largest processor of patients data in africa, we enable healthcare institutions and the government make real-time, data-driven decisions to deliver personalized patient care, and in parallel, facilitate sustainability, development, and delivery of optimized medical care through our distinctive solutions and products

Fully incorporated in 2014, we have deep industry expertise and experience in empowering healthcare service providers to render services to their patients in a sustainable, consistent, scalable and cost-effective manner.

We believe healthcare can become more resilient and innovative with digitally enabled business models with software and intelligence at the core. Today, we are transforming effective delivery of health Insurance through; identifying the actual cost of healthcare delivery, transparent pre-determination of billing and premium bases and transparent delivery of health insurance services in the healthcare ecosystem We have built an unrivalled reputation in delivering superior innovative solutions thus putting us in a strong position to streamline and provide regulators with tools for compliance/ ethics in healthcare provisioning and a framework for efficient healthcare supply chain management (drug/medical supply chain)

Our track record of excellent execution in deploying technologies that enhances effective delivery of healthcare services has yielded amazing results in over 17 tertiary health institutions with full internal process optimization/automation and revenue assurance.
